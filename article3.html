rockstar games announces new kortz center heist for gta online.

with gta 6 around the corner rockstar has announced the kortz center heist for gta online. teaming up with mr faber and raf you are able to buy your very own art studio in your mansion to steal the precious artworks of the kortz center. you can go by yourself or up to 3 other players to rob the kortz center with it having the most amount of approaches out of any heist in the game this is the 1st big heist in the game since cayo perico released in 2020 players are understandably very hyped as they will not only be able to go for a big heist once again but have a storyline with mr faber potentially with many possibilities only time will tell what will happen but knowing rockstar games they will surely deliver a fun experience for gamers.
