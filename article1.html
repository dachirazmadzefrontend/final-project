Xbox lays off 1600 employees with more confirmed to be laid off later.

Recently 1600 employees were laid off from xbox as part of the current CEO asha sharmas restructure for xbox after the previous CEO phil spencer ended up nearly destroying the company. asha sharma has stated that with the cards she was dealt with this was the best possible option for the company to stay afloat and that 1600 more people will be laid off in the follwoing months.

This sounds horrible however there is a silver lining to this as none of the studios that lost employees or were fired from xbox are closing down but rather going independent meaning the games they are making are not cancelled. in addition asha sharma has stated that she wants to focus on the games that make them the most money rather than taking that money from them and investing it into other games this means studios like mojang and bungie will no longer have issues with money. in addition they will report directly to her for ideas rather than going through 10 layers of management which should hopefully improve update times for their games. However for minecraft it could mean implementing monetization into java edition which nobody likes only time will tell what could potentially happen with these changes.
