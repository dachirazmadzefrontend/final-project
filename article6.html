gta 6 pre orders announced.

after 13 years of gta 5 the time has finally come to leave los santos and go to vice city with the gta 6 pre orders being dropped rockstar has made an estimated 3 billion dollars from pre orders alone. However theres a controversy happening about the standard edition and ultimate edition of the game as the ultimate edition seems to include some shops and activities that arent obtainable in the standard edition.

Despite the controversy rockstar games has still made lots of money from this game now its all a matter of waiting for the release of the game to see if it will live up to peoples expectations or be the greatest flop in gaming history.
