xbox announces a way to help physical games.

the gaming company xbox following playstations controvesial move to cancel phsyical games announced something that woul instead help physical games. Now if you buy a physical game once you insert the disc once you get a permanent digital license for the game meaning you dont have to insert it again to play it however the disc itself will still remain functional even after the one use. This means that you dont have to insert the game disc every time to play the game eliminating an annoyance with physical games.

This move by xbox was a smart one to gain consumers and capitalize off of playstations controversial move to cancel physical games if xbox keeps going like this they might just win the console wars.
